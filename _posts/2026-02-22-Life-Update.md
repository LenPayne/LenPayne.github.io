# The Return to Christ (and Church)

> But Len, this site is all about tech stuff?! What are you doing?!

Well, hypothetical audience, I always try my best to be a whole and authentic 
person. I will try to keep my separate parts separate for the sake of my 
audiences (eg- I will probably build a Substack for religious writings that 
I'm not seeking to publish elsewhere) but it's been a long six years and this is the biggest change in my life, so I am sharing it.

In short, Katie started going back to church in 2017: that year we lost my Mom,
and she lost a friend from high school. That brush with death helped her 
realize there was more to life. Over the following years, I joined her.

I've run out of steam. Today, Katie and I are both really sick. We've been 
fighting off the flu in one form or another since New Years. I may edit this 
more later, but I'm going to publish in case I don't.
