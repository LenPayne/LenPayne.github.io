Summary

Len Payne is a Software Developer, IT Professional and Coach in Sarnia, Ontario, Canada. He is currently the Chief Technology Officer at Link2Feed, a SaaS provider based out of Sarnia and Detroit, which helps food banks and other poverty-relief organizations in managing their caseloads. Through Lambton College, Len teaches a variety of courses from systems administration to software development and project management. Len also actively participates in Applied Research at the College, as well as entrepreneurship activities through The Cube.
