Summary

Len Payne is a Software Developer, IT Professional and Coach in Sarnia, Ontario, Canada. He is currently the Chief Technology Officer at Link2Feed, a SaaS provider based out of Sarnia and Detroit, which helps food banks and other poverty-relief organizations in managing their caseloads. Through Lambton College, Len has taught a variety of courses from systems administration to software development and project management. Len also actively participates in Applied Research at the College, as well as entrepreneurship activities through Cube and The Sarnia Tech Community. Len is a proud founder of the Sarnia-Lambton Linux Users Group.
